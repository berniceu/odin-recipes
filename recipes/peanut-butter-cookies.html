Homemade peanut butter cookies

Who doesn't like cookies? Here is an easy home recipe for killer peanut butter cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter

Steps

  1. Add cream shortening, peanut butter, and both sugars in a large bowl and mix with electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in egg until incorporated. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in separate bowl.
  3. Gradually stir into peanut butter mixture until combined. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour
  4. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius
  5. Roll chilled dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2 inches apart onto baking sheets
  6. Flatten dough balls with a fork dipped in flour to form a crosswise pattern(optional)
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set, 10 t0 12 minutes.
  8. Transfer cookies to a wire rack to cool completely
